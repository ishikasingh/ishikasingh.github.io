Ishika Singh

Undergrad, Computer Science majors

IIT Kanpur

I am a final year undergrad at IIT Kanpur. I am fortunate to have worked with Prof. Ashutosh Modi and Prof. Nisheeth Srivastava at IITK. I’ve also had the pleasure of being supervised by Prof. Pengtao Xie as a research intern at UC San Diego. I’ve spent one of my summers interning at Adobe Research. I’ve mostly worked in NLP and theoretical ML. My research interests are across NLP, RL, ML theory; primarily around why (how) language is necessary for (can be utilized in) robot learning.

I am also a part of Brain & Cognitive Society (BCS) at IIT Kanpur, currently leading the group, where I organize interesting talks and collaborate/mentor projects. I have also been an Academic Mentor to freshers for 1st year Mathematics Courses offered at IIT Kanpur.

In my leisure time, I participated in a competition ( TechnoUtsav 2.0) organized by Deloitte, and my idea on “Intelligent Energy Harvesting” won the Grand Prize. My team was invited to US India Analytics Summit 2019, where I met my youngest inspiration Tanmay Bakshi. All of my efforts were appreciated by Adobe Research, and I am very grateful to them that they selected me for Adobe India WIT 2020 Scholarship. Special mention: I have spent sometime in electrochemical research as well under Prof. Raj Pala. He has been a constant source of encouragement and knowledge (and fresh/smart jokes :P) to me.

(I am cooler than you think :P) I also spend time writing about my philosophical thoughts and have a predilection towards painting (traditional art). To know more about me, or my projects, or hire me, or to just chat, contact on your favorite interface from the menu on your left. I’m most efficient with emails and twitter.
